Crepes

Ingredients


  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tbs butter

Directions

  1. Mix ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a medium sized pan.
  3. Add the mixture to the heated oil. Cook until the bubbles slow or stop then flip to cook the other side.
